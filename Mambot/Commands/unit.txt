.acr "!unit" %user% typed `!unit`. This provides an overview of how to purchase all units.

Most units can be bought with :ef_medal:, the __**exceptions**__ are the 12 following units:

Released in Korea, unavailable in International at this time.
```
Cleric       Human   Magical    [Buffs Orc as well   ]
Shaman       Elf     Physical   [Buffs Human as well ]
Dark Elf     Undead  Physical   [Buffs Elf as well   ]
Frost Demon  Orc     Physical   [Buffs Undead as well]
```
Available from __Honor Shop__ for :ef_hc: 4400 (released 07/06/2017)
```
Fire Spirit    Human   Magical
Hippogryph     Elf     Physical
Dark Spirit    Undead  Magical
Blade Master   Orc     Physical
```
Available from __Unit Shop__  for :ef_gem: 5700
```
Valkyrie       Human,  Physical
Sword Dancer   Elf     Magical
Incubus        Undead  Magical
Ice Spirit     Orc     Magical
```

When the next units are released they will be in the honor shop. The current honor units become available for gems and the current gem units become available for medals.

**Any 5-star unit without exception can appear in 5-star Voucher**
